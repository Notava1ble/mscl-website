---
title: How are points earned?
description: An overview of MSCL's scoring system.
---

Points are calculated based on your placement in each match. The exact calculation is as follows:

> _points = totalPlayers - placement + 1_

Where `totalPlayers` is the total number of players in the match, and `placement` is your rank in the match (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.). If a player doesn't finish the match, they will be given 0 points.

Here is how it works, if there are 5 players in a match and the last two didn't finish:

- 1st Place: 5 - 1 + 1 = 5 points
- 2nd Place: 5 - 2 + 1 = 4 points
- 3rd Place: 5 - 3 + 1 = 3 points
- 4th Place: dnf = 0 points
- 5th Place: dnf = 0 points

Total points at the end of the week determine promotions and relegations.
