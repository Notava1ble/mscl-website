---
title: Relegations and Promotions
description: How players move between leagues.
---

At the end of each week, we promote and relegate players to ensure that the leagues are balanced and that the competition is fair.

## How it works

Based on the total points earned by each player in a week, we promote and relegate players. The exact number of players promoted and relegated is based on the number of players in each league. Currently, we promote top 10% of players from each league and relegate bottom 10% of players from each league.

## Tie breaks

If at the end of all matches, there is a tie for the last spot for promotion or relegation, another match will be played between the tied players to determine the promotion/relegation.
