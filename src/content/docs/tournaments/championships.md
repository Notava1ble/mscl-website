title: League Championships (Leagues 2-6)
description: Information regarding championships for non league 1 leagues.
---

The Championships are events for Leagues 2–6, with a smaller cash prize aimed at players not in the top 0.1%
The current prize pool is $100 USD so $15 for first and $5 for second (per league)

Each league runs its own separate Championship using the same overall structure but with different numbers of seeds, to fit a ~2 hour time limit per league. The format is ‘survival knockout’: all remaining players play every seed in the round; after each round a set number of players are eliminated.

Distribution of seeds and knockouts are as follows:

| League | Time Limit (from 1st completion) | No. of Seeds | Percentage Eliminated |
| ------ | -------------------------------- | ------------ | --------------------- |
| 2      | +5                               | 8            | 12%                   |
| 3      | +7                               | 7            | 13%                   |
| 4      | +10                              | 6            | 16%                   |
| 5      | +15                              | 5            | 20%                   |
| 6      | +20                              | 4            | 26%                   |

The final seed will have 20% of the initial players left, and the top 2 will win prizes. Each league will have a different percentage of people eliminated each round.

E.g League 3 will have 16% of people eliminated each round, **ROUNDED DOWN**, to have 20% remaining at the end. If you DNF or don’t complete within the time limit, you will be eliminated. This 16% includes the people who didnt complete, so if you complete and 16% of the lobby didn't you would survive. If more people DNF then percentage of people eliminated each round, the DNF rule will take priority. So if 20% of people DNF then 20% of people will get eliminated. (Doesnt affect no of eliminations the next round either)

In the final seed top 2 secure first and second based on completion of that seed alone.

## Anti-Smurfing
You will only be able to win prizes for the highest league youve played in (so deranking from league 2 to 5 means you can still only win prizes for league 2) and you will only be able to win a prize for each league ONCE. Winners will be promoted to the league above anyway too. We employ strict anti-smurfing when dealing with signups and handle things case by case. You will need to have played in a league once before competing and follow the same rules as ranked. Anyone caught smurfing will be perma-banned with option to appeal.

NOTE: These seeds will be ranked filter intended seeds. They are not designed to be always linear and there will be harder to play seeds, just like in playoffs. The only seeds that we wont have in are incompletable seeds or seeds where the ranked filter makes a mistake.
