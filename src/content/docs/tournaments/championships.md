The Championships are end‑of‑season events for Leagues 2–6.

Each league runs its own separate Championship using the same overall structure but with different numbers of seeds, to fit a ~2 hour time limit per league. Format is ‘survival knockout’: all remaining players play every seed in the round; after each round a set number of players are eliminated.

Distribution of seeds and knockouts are as follows:

| League | Time Limit (from 1st completion) | No. of Seeds |
| ------ | -------------------------------- | ------------ |
| 2      | +5                               | 8            |
| 3      | +7                               | 7            |
| 4      | +10                              | 6            |
| 5      | +15                              | 5            |
| 6      | +20                              | 4            |

The final seed will have 20% of the initial players left, and the top 2 will win prizes. Each league will have a different percentage of people eliminated each round.
